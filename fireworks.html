Drone Video of Fireworks


Happy Fourth of July! On Friday, June 30, 2017, the Philly By Drone team shot drone cinematography of the Welcome America fireworks on the Delaware River. Fireworks were launched by Pyrotecnico off of a barge on the Delaware River.

As our gift to the Philadelphia community to commemorate our nation's 241st anniversary, we've made our drone video public for all to enjoy. Below is additional information if you would like to obtain the original video file to post onto your own accounts.
