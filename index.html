About

Philly By Drone specializes in aerial real estate photography in Philadelphia. Christopher Kao is an FAA Part 107 certified commercial drone pilot. Our past clients have included the University of Pennsylvania, Scion Group, Irvine Company, Yale University, Goldenberg Development, Curbed Philly, Sotheby's Realty, Philly Inquirer, NBC10, and Keller Williams Realty.

Our aerial photography package starts at $250, and varies depending on the size of your building. We will come out to your location, spend about an hour shooting as many photos, edit the photos, and get them to you in a Google Drive folder, all within 24 hours. We also shoot videos as well. Email us at phillybydrone@gmail.com or give us a call at (714) 855-5951.
