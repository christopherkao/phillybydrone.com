About


Philly By Drone specializes in drone real estate photography in Philadelphia. We are FAA certified, have drone insurance liability coverage, and operate as an LLC.

Our past clients include Goldenberg Development, Alliance Partners HSP, HFF, National Real Estate Development, University of Pennsylvania, Drexel University, Hyatt Hotels, and many more. Our residential photography package starts at $350, and commercial photography package starts at $750.

Email us at phillybydrone@gmail.com or give us a call at (714) 855-5951 for a custom quote.
