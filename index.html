About

Philly by Drone specializes in aerial real estate photography. Christopher Kao is an FAA Part 107 certified commercial drone pilot and flies the DJI Phantom 4. Our past clients have included the University of Pennsylvania, Scion Group, Irvine Company, Yale University, and Curbed Philly.

Our aerial photography package is $250. We will come out to your location, spend about an hour shooting, edit the photos, and get them to you in a Google Drive folder, all within 24 hours. Email us at chriskao@seas.upenn.edu or give us a call at (714) 855-5951.
