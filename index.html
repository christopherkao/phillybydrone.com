About

Philly By Drone specializes in aerial real estate photography in Philadelphia. Christopher Kao is an FAA Part 107 certified commercial drone pilot. Our past clients have included Goldenberg Development, Scion Group, University of Pennsylvania, Drexel University, Sotheby's Realty, Keller Williams Realty, Erdy McHenry Architecture, and Curbed Philly.

Our residential photography package starts at $250, and commercial photography package starts at $500. We do a lot of research, and often come out to the property beforehand to survey the surrounding area. On the day of the shoot, we'll take photos from all angles. Afterwards, we spend quality time editing and upload the photos to a Google Drive folder for you within a timely manner. Email us at phillybydrone@gmail.com or give us a call at (714) 855-5951 for a custom quote.
